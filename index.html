We collect your email address for account creation and authentication. We collect photos you choose to upload for homework solving. We do not collect any other personal information.

Photos are sent to our AI service solely to generate homework solutions and are not stored permanently. Your email is used only for login and account management.

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties, except as required by law or to provide core app functionality (e.g., AI processing).

Children's Privacy

StudyBuddy is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Data Retention

You may delete your account at any time from the Profile tab. All associated data will be removed within 30 days.
